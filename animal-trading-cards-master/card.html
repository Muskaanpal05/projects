Akita Dog

The Akita is a bold and willful dog. Akitas have webbed toes to help walk on snow by disturbing their weight more effectively. When the weather turns cooler the dogs seems to have a "turbo" button that switches on.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 66-71 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Origin: Japan
  • Habitat: Snowy, rural, mountainous region
